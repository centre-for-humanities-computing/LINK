REVERIE OVER A DEMI-TASSE

Has a man a soul? 
Is there an After Life? 
How the earth has been twisting about	for these thousands of years! 
And the plants - springing up and dying 
	and springing up again!"Strong Son of " God. . . . Believing	where we cannot prove. . . .". 
What was that conversation now be-
	tween Emerson and the Congress-
	man?And that nihilist, Omar! 
There is a certain infallible regularity	about the universe. 
If Mars is inhabited as they surmise? 
Why didn't Halley's Comet hit us as an	experiment? 
If man has a soul.... 
Waiter, bring me a Colorado perfecto:	one of those with the pink and	white bands.
